Privacy Policy

Curio Media · Last updated: June 26, 2026 · Effective: June 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Curio Media ("Curio Media", "we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with our internal content-automation software (the "Application"). The Application publishes short-form video content that Curio Media creates to Curio Media's own social media accounts (including TikTok and Instagram) using those platforms' official APIs.

1. Scope

The Application is a first-party tool used by Curio Media to manage and publish its own content. It does not provide services to, or collect data from, members of the public. The only account data it accesses is that of the Curio Media accounts that explicitly authorize it.

2. Information We Access and Store

  • Authorization credentials. OAuth access and refresh tokens issued by a platform (e.g. TikTok, Meta/Instagram) when a Curio Media account grants the Application permission to publish on its behalf.
  • Account identifiers. Basic profile information returned by the platform APIs, such as account ID, username, and display name of the authorizing account.
  • Content we publish. The video files, thumbnails, captions, and metadata that Curio Media creates and submits for posting.
  • Post results and metrics. Identifiers and performance metadata (e.g. post ID, publish status, view/engagement counts) returned by the platform after a post is created.

3. How We Use Information

We use the information solely to: (a) authenticate with the platform APIs; (b) publish and schedule Curio Media's own content; (c) confirm posting status and retrieve performance metrics for our content; and (d) operate, maintain, and debug the Application. We do not use this information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to publishing our own content.

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade information. We share information only with the platforms whose APIs we use (TikTok, Meta/Instagram) to the extent necessary to publish our content and as governed by their terms. We may disclose information if required by law.

5. Data Storage and Security

Access tokens and related data are stored on systems controlled by Curio Media with access restricted to authorized personnel. Tokens are kept in protected storage (not in public source code) and are transmitted only over encrypted (HTTPS) connections to the platform APIs.

6. Data Retention

We retain authorization tokens only while the Application is authorized to act on an account, and delete them when authorization is revoked. Content and post metadata are retained only as long as needed for our publishing operations and record-keeping, then deleted.

7. Your Choices and Revocation

Authorization can be revoked at any time from the relevant platform's connected-apps settings (e.g. TikTok or Instagram account settings). Revoking access stops the Application from acting on that account and triggers deletion of the associated tokens.

8. Children's Privacy

The Application is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect information from anyone under 13 years of age.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above and posting the revised Policy at this URL.
